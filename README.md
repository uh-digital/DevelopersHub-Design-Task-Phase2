# DevelopersHub Corporations - Design Task (Phase 2)

Welcome to my repository for Phase 2 of the Graphic Design Internship at **DevelopersHub Corporations**. This repository contains all the design deliverables completed during Week 1 and Week 2, showcasing brand identity development and website UI design.

## 👤 Intern Information
- **Role:** Graphic Design Intern
- **Phase:** 02
- **Duration:** Week 1 & Week 2

---

## 📅 Weekly Deliverables & Project Breakdown

### 🎨 Week 1 – Brand Identity & Social Media Design
**Objective:** Build a solid visual identity and launch-ready social media content.

*   **Tasks Completed:**
    *   **Services & Hiring Posts:** Created 5 Instagram posts (1080x1080px) including 3 service posts (Web Development, App Development, UI/UX Design) and 2 hiring/internship posts with clear CTAs and realistic mockups.
    *   **Logo Design:** Developed 3 unique logo options (icon + text) with multiple variations (icon-only, horizontal, vertical, black & white, and tagline versions).
    *   **Brand Guidelines Booklet:** Compiled a comprehensive guide covering logo rules, color palettes (HEX, RGB, CMYK), typography, icon styles, post examples, and brand mockups (business card, t-shirt, app icon).
*   **Deliverables Included:**
    *   📁 Final Logo Files
    *   📄 Brand Guidelines PDF
    *   🖼️ 5 Social Media Posts

---

### 💻 Week 2 – Website UI Design
**Objective:** Apply the newly created branding to a clean, user-friendly desktop website layout.

*   **Tasks Completed:**
    *   **5-Page Website UI Design:** Designed high-fidelity desktop screens focusing on consistent branding, typography, clear visual hierarchy, spacing, and responsive layout considerations.
    *   **Pages Designed:**
        1. Home Page
        2. About Us
        3. Services
        4. Internship / Join Us
        5. Contact
    *   **Review & Refinement:** Applied stakeholder feedback to finalize development-ready screens.
*   **Deliverables Included:**
    *   🌐 Website UI Screens (Desktop-first approach)

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

*   **Figma:** Used for designing the 5 Instagram Social Media Posts and the complete 5-Page Website UI Design.
*   **Canva:** Used for creating the 3 Logo Options and formatting the complete Brand Guidelines Booklet/PDF.
*   **GitHub:** Used for version control and final design submission.
